What We Offer

We believe great food begins with family. Our recipes are more than just meals—they’re memories, traditions, and stories from our table to yours.

At Pollo Locuas, we are proud to offer a wide range of services designed to create the perfect dining and entertainment experience for every guest. Whether you're looking to enjoy a relaxing meal in our warm and inviting dining area or sip on expertly crafted cocktails at our fully stocked alcohol bar, we provide the ideal setting for any occasion. For those on the go, our convenient take-in and delivery services bring the authentic flavors of our kitchen right to your doorstep. Planning a special event? Our catering service ensures your gathering is memorable with delicious, handcrafted dishes tailored to your needs. And for those seeking a little excitement, our gambling area adds an extra layer of fun and entertainment to your visit.

  • Taco Cart

    A mobile food vendor offering fresh, made-to-order tacos with various meat, seafood, and vegetarian fillings. Typically includes toppings like onions, cilantro, and salsa, served from a compact cart at events, streets, or food parks.

  • Food Trays

    Fresh, generous portions arranged in professional catering trays: rice, beans, seasoned meats, grilled vegetables, warm tortillas, fresh salsas, and all toppings. Perfect for events, complete with utensils and serving equipment.

  • Chips and Salsa

    House-made tortilla chips, fried daily until golden and crispy, served with your choice of fresh salsas: mild pico de gallo, smoky chipotle, zesty tomatillo verde, and spicy habanero roja.

  • Various Meats

    Choose from tender carne asada (grilled beef), juicy al pastor (marinated pork), flavorful pollo (chicken), succulent pescado (fish), spicy chorizo (Mexican sausage), or traditional lengua (beef tongue). Each prepared fresh on our flat-top grill.

  • Elote Cart

    Fresh corn on the cob grilled to perfection, slathered with mayo, rolled in cotija cheese, chili powder, and lime. Served on a stick or in a cup as esquites. Made-to-order from our street-style cart.

  • Ice-Cream Cart

    Authentic Mexican ice cream and frozen treats served from a vibrant pushcart. Features flavors like mango, coconut, and Mexican vanilla, plus paletas (popsicles), chamoyadas (fruit slush), and traditional corn-based treats.
